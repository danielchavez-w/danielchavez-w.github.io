About

Built in solitude. Shipped to the world.

I'm a self-taught developer from Chicago. I gained my skills through years of disciplined, independent study — learning by designing, building, and shipping complete systems: an AI code tutor, a multi-device voice assistant, a network security monitor, and two games.

I work iteratively — one problem at a time, documented as I go — and I own every layer of what I build: the architecture, the debugging, the trade-offs, and the engineering decisions behind them. AI tools like Claude Code multiply my productivity, but the systems thinking and judgment driving each project are mine, built the slow way: by shipping.

Open to entry-level and early-career software engineering, AI application development, automation, and technical support engineering opportunities.
